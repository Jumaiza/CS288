    What is Seibon Carbon?

    Seibon Carbon, a division of Seibon International, Inc., was founded by automotive aficionados in 2003. Their goal was, and still is, to provide the highest quality carbon fiber auto body parts and exceptional customer service. Seibon Carbon specializes in the design and manufacture of top-grade carbon fiber and dry carbon automotive body components, including hoods, fenders, trunks, doors, and aerodynamic body additions like front lip kits, side skirts, and rear spoilers. 

    Why Choose Seibon Carbon?

    Seibon’s very own in-house design team introduces and preserves proprietary designs for a wide array of sport compact applications. Each individual Siebon product boasts lightweight, style, durability, and functionality. Everything is crafted from top-quality carbon fiber and rigorously inspected and tested during the company’s multi-step quality control process. All Seibon hoods and carbon fiber body kits are made with a consistent weave pattern and exceptional carbon/resin ration for added strength and stiffness. The lustrous shine comes from Seibon’s glossy clear coat that is guaranteed to not chip or flake.

    Seibon understands the correlation between continued growth and pleased customers. For that reason, the brand constantly strives for accurate and rapid turnaround rates on all orders. Seibon backs its products with a “clear-coat warranty” and “fitment guarantee” that ensures confidence and satisfaction. The Seibon team has and continues to work tirelessly around the clock to bring only the best products to the auto industry. They are dedicated to using the latest in advanced technology and keeping suggestions in mind as to continue meeting the needs of such an ever-changing field.

    Seibon Carbon is Worldwide

    Seibon’s carbon components have been featured in a number of television shows, in movies, on magazine covers, at racing events, and at regional and national auto shows. Above all, Seibon carbon fiber parts continue to be in high demand by knowledgeable car enthusiasts worldwide. With active dealers in the U.S., Canada, United Kingdom, Korea, Russia, Singapore, Dubai, Switzerland, and more, Seibon continues to successfully compete on a global level. Seibon is also very proud to be a member of the Specialty Equipment Manufacturers Association and participate in the SEMA show annually.
